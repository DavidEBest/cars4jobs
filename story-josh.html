Josh's Story

Resilience in Motion

Josh went through a difficult season that disrupted his stability and set him back in ways he never expected.

Determined to rebuild, he made the decision to seek help and commit to a structured inpatient program. Completing that program marked a turning point, one where he chose growth, accountability, and a fresh start.

From there, Josh connected with The Equity Center, where he gained skills, training, and meaningful employment. Through their support and his own determination, he completed the program and secured a position with a local manufacturing company. For the first time in years, he had steady employment and a renewed sense of purpose.

What he still didn't have was reliable transportation.

Josh had not owned a vehicle in over five years. Without a car, maintaining work, managing appointments, and handling daily responsibilities required extra time, planning, and stress.

When Impact Garage re-homed a dependable vehicle to Josh, it represented freedom and stability.

Today, Josh isn't defined by his past challenges. He's defined by resilience, growth, and momentum — and now, he has the reliable transportation to keep moving forward.
