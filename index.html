About Cars4Work

Cars4Work is an initiative of three Dayton-area 501(c)3 non-profit organizations: The Collaboratory, The Equity Center, and Impact Garage. Cars4Work will be operated by Impact Garage.

Problem Statements

Manufacturing and logistics employers face persistent workforce challenges driven by transportation barriers:

Missed Shifts

Missed shifts and tardiness due to unreliable transit.

High Turnover

High turnover among frontline and entry-level workers.

Limited Hiring

Limited hiring radius in car-dependent regions.

Lost Productivity

Lost productivity and increased training costs.

For individuals, the difference between keeping a job and losing one has nothing to do with effort — it has everything to do with access. A lack of reliable transportation often blocks accepting a job, advancement, and long-term financial stability.

What We Do

Cars4Work partners with qualified employers and workforce development programs to identify and assist individuals whose employability/employment status is dependent on having reliable, affordable transportation. The process is intentionally structured and employer-supported, ensuring accountability and long-term success. The result is reduced absenteeism, improved retention, and expansion of the available labor pool.

Long-Term Vision

Cars4Work becomes a standard workforce infrastructure program that provides a bridge between employment, mobility, and economic stability.

Our Collaborators

Cars4Work brings together three organizations committed to workforce development and economic mobility.

Impact Garage

A non-profit organization whose goal is to relieve a financial burden by providing reliable, low-cost cars and low-cost car repairs to individuals working to lift themselves out of poverty. Impact Garage serves as the program operator and is responsible for:

  • Vehicle Sourcing Strategy and Implementation (accept donations, dealer auction purchases, refurbish vehicles)
  • Title transfer, compliance frameworks, and tax donation statements
  • Measurement of car donation counts and rehoming

Visit Impact Garage →

The Equity Center

Empowers individuals and communities by providing access to resources, education, and support. Through their workforce development programs, The Equity Center:

  • Identifying and supporting clients in need of affordable, reliable transportation to close the employment loop
  • Working with employers to see the value of Cars4Work and become partners in the program
  • Collecting, assembling, and analyzing relevant data from employers

Visit The Equity Center →

The Collaboratory

A non-profit civic innovation lab. It functions as a catalyst, convenor, and connector to co-create and incubate social enterprises. The Collaboratory is responsible for:

  • Building the Cars4Work brand architecture
  • Network and Resource Development
  • Facilitating the development process
