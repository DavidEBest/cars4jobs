Transportation Shouldn't Stand Between You and Your Job

Cars4Work connects employees with reliable, affordable vehicles—reducing absenteeism, improving retention, and treating transportation as essential workforce infrastructure.

About Cars4Work

Cars4Work is an initiative of The Collaboratory, The Equity Center, and Impact Garage, which also serves as the operating organization.

The Problem

Manufacturing and logistics employers face persistent workforce challenges driven by transportation barriers:

Missed Shifts

Unreliable transit leads to tardiness and absences, disrupting operations.

High Turnover

Frontline and entry-level workers leave when they can't reliably get to work.

Limited Hiring

Car-dependent regions restrict the available labor pool to those with vehicles.

Lost Productivity

Constant rehiring means increased training costs and reduced efficiency.

For employees, lack of reliable transportation often blocks job access, advancement, and long-term financial stability.

Our Vision

Cars4Work becomes a standard workforce infrastructure program that provides a bridge between employment, mobility, and economic stability.

Our Collaborators

Cars4Work brings together three organizations committed to workforce development and economic mobility.

Impact Garage

A non-profit organization providing reliable low-cost cars and repairs to individuals working to lift themselves out of poverty. Impact Garage serves as the program operator.

  • Vehicle sourcing (donations, auctions, refurbishment)
  • Title transfer and compliance
  • Tracking donation counts and vehicle rehoming

Visit Impact Garage →

The Equity Center

Empowers individuals and communities through access to resources, education, and support via workforce development programs.

  • Identifying clients needing affordable transportation
  • Partnering with employers to join Cars4Work
  • Collecting and analyzing employer data

Visit The Equity Center →

The Collaboratory

A non-profit civic innovation lab that functions as a catalyst, convenor, and connector to co-create and incubate social enterprises.

  • Building the Cars4Work brand
  • Network and resource development
  • Facilitating the development process
