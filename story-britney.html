Britney's Story

From Survival to Purpose

Britney is rebuilding her life.

As a resident of a local organization supporting single moms, she made the courageous decision to pursue a degree in social work, determined to turn her own hardships into hope for other families.

But between parenting, classes, and responsibilities, unreliable transportation threatened to undo her progress. Getting to school, work, and caring for her child became stressful and uncertain.

Impact Garage re-homed a dependable vehicle to Britney, giving her more than transportation.

It gave her stability. It gave her independence. It gave her momentum.

Today, she's steadily working toward her degree, driving confidently toward a future where her story becomes someone else's support system.

Reliable transportation didn't just help her move. It helped her move forward.
