Lani's Story

The Road to Her Promise

Lani has never taken the easy road.

She learned early how to adapt, how to persevere, and how to keep moving even when the future felt uncertain. She balanced school, part-time jobs, and the emotional weight of building a future largely on her own.

College wasn't just a goal. It was a promise she made to herself that her story would be defined by where she was going, not where she had been.

But there was one obstacle standing in her way: reliable transportation.

Through Impact Garage, a vehicle was re-homed to Lani. It wasn't brand new. It had miles on it and a story of its own. But it was dependable, carefully inspected, and repaired by technicians and volunteers who believe reliable transportation can change lives.

With keys in hand, Lani headed off to college, the beginning of her next chapter and the future she's worked so hard to build.

Because sometimes the difference between a dream and a degree is simply a way to get there.
